Who We Serve

Pel's Kitchen is a food truck servicing the Central Coast area, serving up comforting Russian dumplings. We want to create a legacy of connecting with the Central Coast community through amazing food and friendly service. As a company founded by Cal Poly Alum, Pel's Kitchen caters to university community members looking for a warm meal at an affordable price at every stop along our route.
